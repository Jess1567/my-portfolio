Born To make History

          Before we get started, let's begin our journey with the history of the Developers Masquerade Ball! The Developers Masquereade Ball was founded November 7, 20XX by Cielo Verity. Its mission was to bring young developers from ages 18-25 together to showcase their creations to companies looking to hire, as well as young developers getting to network with each other in elegance, class, and style. This ball has bloomed into a night of youth, mystery, code, and wonder. With the combination of dance, code, and art showcases, The Developer's Masquereade Ball was born. The location changes every year between San Diego, Miami Flordia, New York City, Seattle Washingtion, Atlanta Georgia, and more locations will continue to develop as we continue to progress.
